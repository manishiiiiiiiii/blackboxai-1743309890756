Frequently Asked Questions

How do I modify or cancel my reservation?

You can modify or cancel your reservation by calling our reservations team or through the confirmation email you received.

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Standard check-in is at 2:00 PM and check-out is at 12:00 PM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request.

Do you have wheelchair accessible rooms?

Yes, most of our properties have wheelchair accessible rooms. Please specify this requirement when making your reservation.
