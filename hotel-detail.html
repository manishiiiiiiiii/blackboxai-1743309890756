Iconic Luxury in Mumbai

The Taj Mahal Palace, Mumbai is India's first harbour landmark and a global icon of Indian hospitality. Since its opening in 1903, this legendary hotel has hosted kings, dignitaries and eminent personalities from across the globe, and is acknowledged as a world leader in hospitality.

Overlooking the majestic Gateway of India, this luxury hotel's unique architecture offers the finest views of Mumbai's harbour. The hotel combines tradition with modern conveniences to bring you the best of both worlds.
